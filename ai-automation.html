AI Voice Agents

Your 24/7 Front Line

Imagine a team member who never sleeps, never misses a call, and always knows exactly what to say.

SYNAPTIV's AI voice agents are conversational AI systems deployed on your inbound phone lines, website, or messaging channels. They handle enquiries, qualify leads, collect information, book appointments, and escalate complex cases to your human team — all without requiring a single person to pick up the phone.

Built on leading AI voice platforms and customised to your business, our voice agents are trained on your products, your tone, and your processes. They integrate with your CRM, calendar, and communication tools — creating a seamless experience for customers and a dramatically reduced workload for your team.

Use Cases
  • Inbound enquiry handling and qualification for real estate, legal, medical, and professional services
  • Lead capture, follow-up, and appointment booking for sales and service businesses
  • After-hours customer support for businesses that can't staff a 24/7 team
  • Outbound appointment reminders and confirmation calls
  • Client intake workflows for professional services practices

Marketing Automation

Grow Your Revenue on Autopilot

The best marketing doesn't happen when your team is available. It happens every time a potential customer takes an action — visiting your website, opening an email, clicking an ad, or asking a question.

SYNAPTIV's marketing automation solutions turn every customer touchpoint into a personalised, intelligent interaction — nurturing leads, converting prospects, and retaining customers without your team lifting a finger.
