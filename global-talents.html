Overview

Beyond Recruitment.
A Fully Managed Talent Solution.

The offshore staffing industry has a problem. Businesses are handed candidates, left to train them, manage them, and hope they stay. When things go wrong — and they often do — there is no support structure, no accountability, and no recourse.

SYNAPTIV built a different model.

We source, vet, onboard, and continuously manage global professionals as an extension of your business. Our Australian-led management layer ensures that every team member works to your standards, communicates clearly, and delivers the outcomes your business depends on.

We don't just fill roles. We build teams that perform. Our global talent network spans three core disciplines — Accounting & Finance, Engineering Services, and Digital Services — each with its own talent pipeline, technical screening process, and onboarding framework.

Our Process

How We Build
Your Global Team

01

Requirement Intake

We run a structured intake session to define roles, skills, work hours, systems, KPIs, and cultural expectations. This brief becomes the foundation of everything we do.

02

Talent Sourcing

We search our pre-existing talent network first. If we need to source externally, we do so through our established recruitment channels in India, the Philippines, and Sri Lanka.

03

Technical Screening

Candidates complete role-specific technical assessments, English proficiency testing, and background checks. Only those who pass our standard proceed to client interview.

04

Client Interview & Selection

You interview shortlisted candidates and make the final selection. We facilitate the process and provide our recommendation based on technical and cultural fit.

05

Onboarding & Integration

We manage onboarding into your systems, tools, and workflows. Our management team provides ongoing support to ensure a smooth and fast ramp-up.

06

Managed Ongoing Support

Regular check-ins, performance reviews, and proactive management mean your offshore team continuously improves — with no micromanagement required from you.
