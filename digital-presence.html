Overview

Your Digital Environment Should Be
a Competitive Advantage

Too many Australian businesses are running on digital tools that are misconfigured, underutilised, or simply not fit for purpose. Email systems that aren't secure. File storage that isn't structured. CRM data that no one trusts. Collaboration tools that nobody uses.

The result is wasted productivity, security risk, and a workforce that spends more time working around their tools than with them.

SYNAPTIV's Digital Presence practice solves this problem. We implement, migrate, and manage the three most powerful platform ecosystems available to modern businesses — Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and Zoho.

And unlike a one-time implementation project, we stay engaged. Our managed services model means your digital environment is continuously maintained, optimised, and scaled as your business grows.

Microsoft 365

The Enterprise Standard, Delivered Right

Microsoft 365 is the productivity backbone of over one million Australian businesses. But most organisations are only using a fraction of what it can do. SYNAPTIV provides end-to-end Microsoft 365 services — from initial licensing and migration through to advanced deployment of Teams, SharePoint, Power Automate, and the full Microsoft suite.

We configure Microsoft 365 environments that are secure, structured, and built for collaboration — so your team can work from anywhere, on any device, without friction.

Services Include
  • Microsoft 365 tenant setup and licensing management
  • Email migration from Google, on-premise Exchange, or legacy systems
  • SharePoint Online intranet and document management design
  • Microsoft Teams deployment and governance
  • OneDrive for Business configuration and data migration
  • Microsoft Intune device management setup
  • Power Automate workflow integration
  • Security baselines and compliance configuration (MFA, Conditional Access, DLP)
  • Ongoing Microsoft 365 managed support

Google Workspace

Built for Collaboration at Scale

Google Workspace (formerly G Suite) is the preferred platform for teams that live in the browser and prize seamless, real-time collaboration. From Gmail and Drive to Docs, Sheets, Meet, and beyond — Google Workspace is a complete cloud-native productivity environment.

SYNAPTIV delivers Google Workspace implementations tailored to your team's workflow — configuring the tools your people will actually use, securing the data your business depends on, and training your team to unlock the full value of the platform.

Services Include
  • Google Workspace tenant setup and domain verification
  • Email migration from Microsoft 365, on-premise, or legacy email systems
  • Google Drive shared drive structure design and data migration
  • Google Meet and Chat deployment for team communication
  • Admin console configuration, security settings, and user management
  • Google Workspace security review and compliance configuration
  • Ongoing Google Workspace managed support and user training

Zoho Applications

The Business Operating System for Growing Companies

Zoho is one of the most comprehensive business application ecosystems available — spanning CRM, finance, HR, project management, marketing, and more. For growing Australian businesses seeking an integrated, cost-effective alternative to multiple point solutions, Zoho is transformative.

SYNAPTIV implements and customises Zoho applications to match your business processes — eliminating manual workarounds, connecting your data, and giving every team member the tools they need to be productive.

Zoho Applications We Implement
  • Zoho CRM — sales pipeline, lead management, and customer relationship management
  • Zoho Books — cloud accounting, invoicing, and financial reporting
  • Zoho People — HR management, leave, and performance tracking
  • Zoho Projects — project planning, task management, and team collaboration
  • Zoho Desk — customer support ticketing and helpdesk management
  • Zoho Campaigns — email marketing and campaign automation
  • Zoho One — the full Zoho business suite, integrated and configured for your organisation
